Valhalla's
Victory Roast

A majestic roast, marinated in mead and wild herbs, embodying the spirit of Valhalla. Fit for warriors returning from battle.

Ingredients

  • 1 large beef roast (about 5 pounds)
  • 2 cups Mead
  • 1/4 cup whale fat
  • 1/4 cup mixed wild herbs (thyme, rosemary, sage)
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 large onions, quartered
  • 4 carrots, cut into large chunks

Steps

  1. Mix the mead, olive oil, wild herbs, garlic, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  2. Marinate the roast in this mixture overnight in the fridge.
  3. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).
  4. Place the marinated roast in a roasting pan, surrounded by onions and carrots.
  5. Roast for about 3-4 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  6. Let it rest before slicing. Serve with the roasted vegetables.
