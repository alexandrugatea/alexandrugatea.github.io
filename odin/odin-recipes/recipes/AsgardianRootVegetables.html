Asgardian
Root Vegetables

A heavenly side dish featuring root vegetables with a sweet honey glaze, invoking the richness of Asgardian soils.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
  • 2 parsnips, sliced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the sweet potatoes, parsnips, and carrots with olive oil, honey, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Roast for 25-30 minutes, or until tender and caramelized.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
