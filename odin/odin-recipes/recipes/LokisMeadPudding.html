Loki's
Mead Pudding

A sweet, comforting pudding made with berries and mead, topped with a dollop of cream; inspired by the tales of skalds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 2 cups mead
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream for topping
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a saucepan, simmer the berries in mead until they are soft.
  3. Mix the bread crumbs and sugar in a large bowl.
  4. Stir in the beaten eggs and the berry-mead mixture.
  5. Pour into a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes, or until set.
  6. Serve warm with a dollop of heavy cream and mint leaves.
